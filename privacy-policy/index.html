Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

JohnShine Tools provides free online tools for images, PDFs, text, QR codes, and passwords. This policy explains what data we collect, how browser-based tools handle files, and how you can contact us.

Data We Collect

We may collect basic technical and usage information such as pages visited, browser type, device type, approximate location based on IP address, referrer, and interaction events. This helps us understand which tools are useful and improve site reliability.

File and Text Processing

Most tools run directly in your browser. Files are not uploaded to our server unless a tool clearly says otherwise. Image, PDF, and text processing is designed to happen locally on your device where possible.

You should avoid using online tools for files that contain highly sensitive personal, financial, medical, legal, or confidential business information unless you understand how the specific tool handles that data.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to measure traffic and understand how visitors use JohnShine Tools. Google Analytics may use cookies or similar technologies to collect usage data. Google may process this data according to its own privacy policies.

Google AdSense and Advertising

We may use Google AdSense in the future to display ads. Google and other third-party advertising partners may use cookies, advertising identifiers, and similar technologies to serve ads, measure performance, prevent abuse, and personalize advertising where allowed.

Third-party advertisers or ad networks may collect information about your visits to this and other websites. You can manage personalized ad settings through your browser, device settings, or Google's advertising controls.

Cookies

JohnShine Tools may use cookies and similar technologies for analytics, site functionality, security, and future advertising. You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work as expected.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your uploaded files or text content. We may share limited usage or technical data with service providers such as analytics or advertising platforms, as described in this policy, or when required by law.

Contact

For questions, feedback, bug reports, or privacy requests, contact us at support@johnshinetools.com.
